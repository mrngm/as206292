AS206292 / GHCMNET

v20240115

Introduction

AS206292 is an IPv6-only educational network. We currently announce 2001:67c:994::/48 thanks to Netwerkvereniging ColoClue (resource sponsor) and netShelter.

We're planning to announce 2a0c:9a40:8a10::/44. This might be announced in more-specifics (up until /48).

We're planning to announce 81.31.215.0/24.

Peering Policy

AS206292 is open to peering at any location we have a physical or virtual presence. Our PeeringDB record contains the most up to date information regarding those locations (if they are also on PeeringDB), prefix limits, traffic levels, etc.

Peering Locations

We currently peer at:

  • MOSS-IX (AMS, NL), rtr-nlams01.moss-ix.exchanges.as206292.net
  • FogIXP (NIKHEF, NL), rtr-nlams01.fogixp.exchanges.as206292.net
  • FREMIX (Fremont (CA), US), rtr-usfnc01.fremix.exchanges.as206292.net
  • ONIX (Toronto, CA), rtr-cator01.onix.exchanges.as206292.net
  • NL-IX (AMS, NL), rtr-nlams02.nl-ix.exchanges.as206292.net

At the moment, provisioning is done manually. Please drop a line at peering-ghcmnet@moeilijklastig.nl if you wish to peer at any of the above locations.
